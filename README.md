## Character.AI++ — Supercharge Your Character.AI Experience!

Unlock the true potential of Character.AI with **Character.AI++** — the ultimate browser extension designed to give you full control over your chat interface and content!

### Key Features

- **Hide distractions:** Instantly hide annoying UI elements like Top/Trending, AvatarFX, Voices, Scenes, and more — customize your workspace your way.  
- **Export chats:** Save your conversations effortlessly in TXT, JSON, or beautifully styled HTML formats. Perfect for archiving, sharing, or revisiting your best chats anytime!  
- **Bot management:** Easily hide bots or entire bot authors you don’t want to see — keep your feed clean and focused.  
- **Smart redirects:** Automatic redirects to simplify navigation and keep you focused on what matters.  

### Why Character.AI++?

Character.AI++ is made for power users who want a cleaner, more personalized, and more productive Character.AI experience. Whether you're here for fun, roleplay, or creativity, this extension helps you cut through the noise and keep only what you want.

**Try Character.AI++ today and take control of your Character.AI interface like never before!**
